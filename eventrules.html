BID AND DISCUSS

RULE BOOK

1)The participants can participate as an individual or as a team.

2)The size of the team can be up to 3.

3)There are three levels in the event.

Level 1: Ordeal of Perspicuity

This is an Audio and Video Quiz.

The participants are requested to record their answers in the paper given.

At the end of level 1, the participants are awarded credits based on their performance.

The participants can utilize the credits in the upcoming levels.

Level 2: Ideathon

Auctioning of problem statement takes place.

Participants can use their credits to buy problem statements.

A team that fails to buy any problem statement at the end of the auction will be disqualified from the event.

The preparation time allocated for presenting a technical solution for the problem statement is 30 minutes.

The participants can use the charts for preparing the presentation.

The time allocated for the presentation is 2-3 minutes.

This is an elimination round.

Level 3: Eureka

This is a surprise round.

GREEN SCREEN

RULE BOOK

1)It is a team event.

2)The size of the team can be up to 3.

3)There are three levels in the event.

4)Electronic devices are strictly prohibited in the venue.

5)If there is a tie between teams, the winners will be decided based on their quickness.

Level 1: Uncover the Lost

The participants must identify the missing letters on the words displayed on the screen.

The time allocated for each team will be 35 seconds.

Level 2: Connextion

The team should understand a sentence by connecting a set of images.

The time allocated for 3 members team will be 50 seconds and 2 members team will be 1 minute.

Level 3: Grasp the Snap

A team member must visualize the picture and understand the word and act out to his teammates.

The other members have to guess the word without seeing the images.

The time allocated for each team will be 1 min 30 seconds.
